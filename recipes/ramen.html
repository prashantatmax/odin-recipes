Ramen Noodles üçú

A warm, flavorful, and comforting noodle soup made with broth, noodles, vegetables, and a soft-boiled egg.

Recipe Description

Ramen noodles are a popular Japanese-inspired dish, known for their rich broth, chewy noodles and tasty toppings. This homemade version is quick to prepare, customizable and perfect for a filing meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 packs instant noodles (without seasoning)
  • 4 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 2 garlic cloves (chopped)
  • 1 small piece of ginger (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil (or vegetable oil)
  • 1/2 cup mushrooms (sliced)
  • 1/2 spinach or bok choy
  • 2 eggs (soft boiled or boiled)
  • Spring onions (chooped for garnish)
  • Chili flakes (optional)

Steps to Cook

1. Prepare the Broth.

  1. Heat sesame oil in pot.
  2. Add garlic and ginger, saute for 1 minute.
  3. Pour in broth and soy sauce.
  4. Let it simmer for 5-7 minutes.

2. Cook the Noodles

  1. In a separate pot, cook noodles adoring to packing instructions.
  2. Drain and keep aside.

3. Cook the Noodles

  1. Add mushrooms and spinach to the simmering broth.
  2. Cook for 2-3 minutes until softened.
  3. Boil eggs separately (soft-boiled: 6 minutes).

4. Assemble the Ramen & Serve

  1. Place cooked noodles in a bowl.
  2. Pour hot broth with vegetables over them.
  3. Slice the boiled egg and place on top.
  4. Garnish with spring onions and chili flakes.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy immediately.

Your comforting ramen is ready --- Enjoy!!
