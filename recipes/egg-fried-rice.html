A quick, tasty dish made with left over rice, eggs, and veggies.perfect meal for any time.

Recipe Description

Egg fried rice is a classic dish that combine rice with scrambled eggs, veggies, and a touch of soy sauce. it's very easy to prepare yet so delicious and can be customized with your favorite add-ins like chicken or prawns or extra veggies.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of leftover rice
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 small onion (finely chopped)
  • 1/2 cup mixed vegetables (carrot, peas, capsicum, corn, etc)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 spring onions (chopped, for garnish)

Steps to Cook

1. Scramble Eggs

  1. Heat 1 tbsp oil in a pan.
  2. Beat the eggs with a pinch of salt.
  3. Pour into the pan, scramble quickly, and keep aside.

2. Cook Veggies

  1. In the same pan, add another spoon of oil.
  2. Add onion and saute until light golden.
  3. Add mixed vegetables and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes.

3. Mix Rice and Eggs

  1. Add the cooked rice into the pan.
  2. Pour in soy sauce, pepper and a little salt.
  3. Stir well so everything mixes evenly.
  4. Add scrambled eggs back to the pan and mix.

Finish & Serve

  1. Garnish with spring onions.
  2. Serve hot.

Serve hot and enjoy your favorite go-to dish.
