Pizza

This is a simple homemade pizza recipe with a crispy base, flavorful sauce, cheese & toppings.

Recipe Description

Pizza is really fun to prepare and customize according to your preferences. Start with a soft dough, spread tomato sauce, add cheese and finish with vegetables or meats if you like. The result is a fresh, hot and delicious Pizza right from kitchen.

Ingredients:

For the Dough

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp dry yeast
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 3/4 cup warm water

For the Sauce:

  • 1/2 cup tomato puree
  • 1 tsp garlic (chopped or paste)
  • 1/2 oregano
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • salt to taste

For Toppings

  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese (grated)
  • Sliced onion, capsicum, tomato, or mushroom
  • Optional: olives, corn, jalape√±os,cooked chicken, or sausage
